About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is a smart waste management solution designed for modern cities and municipalities. Our mission is to bring transparency, efficiency, and sustainability into waste collection.

What is EcoTrack?

EcoTrack is a web-based smart waste collection management system that enables real-time monitoring of waste collection activities. It supports role-based login (Admin, Collector, User), route optimization, and data-driven insights.

The system is designed to reduce missed pickups, prevent garbage overflow, and promote a cleaner environment through digital tracking and communication between citizens and municipal authorities.

Core Objectives

  • Real-time waste collection tracking via map integration
  • Photo verification of garbage pickup by collectors
  • User complaints and feedback system
  • Eco-friendly performance metrics and reporting
  • Smart scheduling to reduce operational costs
